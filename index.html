DVR/NVR Setup

. We offer complete DVR and NVR setup services to ensure smooth recording and easy monitoring of your CCTV system. Our team configures recording settings based on your storage needs, such as 7 days, 15 days, or 30 days backup. We also set up mobile apps and computer access for remote viewing, allowing you to monitor your property from anywhere. Internet configuration, network settings, and app installation are done securely and professionally. Maintenance & AMC

Regular maintenance is essential for the long-term performance of any CCTV system. PTY Security Solutions offers reliable Maintenance and Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) services to keep your security system running smoothly. Our AMC includes camera inspection, lens cleaning, wiring checks, DVR/NVR health monitoring, and hard disk testing. In case of any issue such as camera failure or recording problems, we provide quick technical support. Regular servicing helps prevent sudden breakdowns and increases the life of the equipment. Our maintenance services are available for residential, commercial, and industrial clients..

Networking

Along with CCTV solutions, we provide professional networking and LAN cabling services. Our services include structured cabling, LAN network setup, rack installation, and secure network configuration. Proper networking ensures stable communication between CCTV cameras, recording devices, and internet connections. We use high-quality cables and follow organized wiring practices for clean installation and easy future maintenance. Secure network setup reduces the risk of unauthorized access and improves overall system reliability. Our networking solutions are suitable for offices, shops, and large commercial installations.
